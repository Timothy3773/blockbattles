About

Battle, Build, and Defeat your opponents in this intense player versus player onslaught. The team to conquer all the lands is crowned victorious. To spice up the combat, everyone is given tools with each tool having its own special abilities. The tools allow you to fight, launch projectiles, drop explosives, and build creatively. Buy better tools and weapons to increase your arsenal and chances of winning! The man behind this is kevo1ution.
